Vision & Mission

Quality Assurance

At SGEES Foodstuff Trading LLC, food safety and quality are ingrained in every aspect of the business operation. Our HACCP certification and compliance with Dubai and UAE regulations demonstrate our commitment to:

Hazard Identification and Control

Our processes identify and control hazards to ensure international standards of food safety and cleanliness in the workplace.

Quality Standards

  • HACCP Accreditation — Food Safety Management System
  • Dubai Municipality Approval — Full compliance
  • Dubai Registration — Food Safety Department

Quality Control Measures

  • Inspection of incoming material by inspection and test
  • Quality checks and monitoring during the process
  • Storage and transportation controls to maintain product integrity
